About Us

Open Weather provides weather data for any location on the globe using a proprietary ML-powered hyperlocal forecasting model with resolution from 500 m to 2 km, globally. While we started as an ethical, non-profit project run by a team of people passionate in making high- quality climate insights more open to the world, these days we focus on complex, enterprise-grade commercial projects.
